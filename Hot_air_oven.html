Hot air oven

HEXAPLAST Hot Air Oven is a laboratory instrument used for sterilization of various materials and equipment using dry heat. It operates by circulating hot air within a closed chamber to maintain a uniform temperature. The oven is commonly used in microbiology, pharmaceuticals, and material testing laboratories for drying, curing, and heat treatment of samples.
