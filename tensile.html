Universal Tensile Testing Machine

HEXAPLAST universal testing machine (UTM) can perform a range of tensile and compression tests to assess the physical properties of the test specimen, implementing internal and external industry-specific standards. This enables cost-efficient quality assurance practices across various industries by offering versatility in conducting multiple tests with a single machine. Here we show how the key factors of flexible software and choice of accessories allow a universal solution to meet unique and specific needs.
