Flexing Endurance Tester

HEXA PLAST Flexing Endurance Tester is a precision testing instrument designed to evaluate the fatigue resistance, crack initiation, and durability of rubber, elastomers, and flexible polymer materials under repeated flexing conditions. The machine simulates continuous bending action to assess material performance and service life. Flexing endurance testing is essential for quality control, material development, and standard compliance in rubber, footwear, automotive components, belts, hoses, and research laboratories.

✔ Smooth and uniform flexing mechanism
✔ Heavy duty and vibration-free construction
✔ Adjustable test parameters for flexibility
✔ User-friendly digital control system

Machine Specifications

  • Test Method : Repeated flexing and fatigue endurance testing
  • Number of Stations : Single / multiple specimen testing options
  • Cycle Counter : Digital counter with preset and auto stop
  • Drive System : Motorized drive with smooth operation
