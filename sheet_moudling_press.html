sheet moudling press

HEXA PLAST Sheet Moulding Press is a heavy-duty hydraulic press designed for moulding, curing, and forming rubber, plastic, and polymer sheet materials under controlled pressure and temperature. The press ensures uniform pressure distribution and precise heating, resulting in high-quality moulded sheets with consistent thickness and properties. Sheet moulding presses are widely used for sample preparation, product development, and production in rubber, polymer processing, automotive, electrical, and research laboratories.
