Gas Permeability Tester

HEXA PLAST Gas Permeability Tester is a precision instrument used to measure the gas transmission rate and permeability of rubber, plastics, films, sheets, and other polymeric materials. The tester evaluates the diffusion of gases such as oxygen, nitrogen, or carbon dioxide through a specimen under controlled temperature and pressure conditions. Gas permeability testing is essential for quality control, material selection, packaging performance, and compliance with international standards in rubber, polymer, packaging, and research laboratories.

✔ Robust and corrosion-resistant construction
✔ Precise temperature and pressure control
✔ User-friendly operation

Machine Specifications

  • Test Method : Gas transmission and permeability measurement as per standard
  • Measurement Range : Suitable for low to high permeability materials
  • Specimen Requirement : Standard films, sheets, or molded samples
  • Test Gas : Oxygen, Nitrogen, CO₂, or custom gases
  • Power Supply : 230V AC
