Compression Tester

HEXA PLAST Compression Tester is a precision testing machine designed to determine the compressive strength, deformation, and load-bearing capacity of materials and products under controlled conditions. It is widely used for quality control, material evaluation, and performance testing of plastics, rubber, packaging materials, corrugated boxes, foam, springs, components, and engineering products. Compression testing is essential to ensure product durability, structural integrity, and compliance with international testing standards in industrial and research laboratories.
